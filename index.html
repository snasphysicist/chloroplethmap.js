UK Heat Map Generator

This is a tool to generate a heatmap of data based on UK postcodes.
Enter your data in the below input box and hit the Generate button to get started.
Data must be in one of the following formats:

postcode1,value1;postcode1,value1; ...

or

postcode1,value1
postcode2,value2
...
